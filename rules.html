Welcome to musicGame

Here are the rules:

At least 4 people if you wanna have fun

Some type of timer, that can be set to between 30-60 seconds

A room with a length of at least 4 meters

How is it played:

Two and two you're gonna compete against other pairs (one pair at a time and stand around 4 meters from eachother) by singing a song from the artist that you see,
if your partner guess the right artist you switch place and coninue like this until your time is up.
Only one person from the team should see the screen with the Artist, so the screen have to be behind of of the persons.

You can do hardcore with around 30 seconds or take a full minute if you like. For every artist you guess you get a score.

Where you play the game you see a white box where you can add your team names and keep track ofr your score. Like this:

Team 1: 3

Team 2: 2

Team 3: 0

If you guess the right artist, a third person should be ready to either click on the New Artist-button or simply just hit 'Enter' on the keyboard. You should also click the button or hit Enter if you don't know the artist or song.

Ps. If you switch between clicking the button and hitting enter it might act a little weird, simply hit refresh and then load you scores again and you are good to go.
